FAQs
What is SPARK ?

SPARK is a one-month long event organized by AIT OSS club, exclusively for the first-year engineering students to introduce them to the field of web development technologies and provide them hands-on experience on web development under the guidance of experienced mentors and a chance to interact with industry professionals.

How many days event ?

SPARK is a one-month long event organized by AIT OSS club

What are the benfits you get from this session ?

1. You will have a chance to kickstart you web devlopment journey completely free of cost. 2. You'll have a chance to learn from experienced menotrs, interact with industry professionals and win exciting prizes 3. You'll have a chance to learn and spread awareness about different open source technologies.

Is there any mentor support for beginner?

24/7 Mentor guidence will be there,you can reach them through Discord server.
